animation-direction

The animation-direction property defines whether an animation should be played forward, backward or in alternate cycles.

Syntax :- animation-direction: normal|reverse|alternate|alternate-reverse|initial|inherit;

alternate :- The animation is played forward first, then backwords

alternate-reverse :- The animation is played backwards first, then forwards
